# Legal Clauses Draft for OnePoint CTO Services

## 1. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure (NDA)

**1.1. Definition of Confidential Information.**
During the course of this Agreement, the Client may disclose to OnePoint CTO ("Provider") certain confidential information, including but not limited to:
*   **System Credentials:** Usernames, passwords, API keys, and access tokens for third-party software (e.g., CRM, Hosting, Banking, Email).
*   **Business Operations:** Customer lists, financial data, marketing strategies, and proprietary business workflows.
*   **Intellectual Property:** Unreleased product concepts, software code, and trade secrets.

**1.2. Obligations.**
Provider agrees to:
*   Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence.
*   Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing the Services (Audits, Implementation, or Management).
*   Take reasonable security precautions to protect the integrity of the Client's systems and data (e.g., using password managers, 2FA).
*   Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the Client’s prior written consent, except as required by law.

**1.3. Exclusions.**
Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach by Provider; (b) was in Provider's possession prior to disclosure; or (c) is independently developed by Provider without use of Client’s Confidential Information.

## 2. Limitation of Liability

**2.1. Indirect Damages.**
To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, in no event shall OnePoint CTO be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, including but not limited to **loss of profits, data, use, goodwill, or other intangible losses**, resulting from:
*   The use or inability to use the implemented systems.
*   Unauthorized access to or alteration of your transmissions or data by third parties.
*   Failures or downtime of third-party services (e.g., OpenAI API outages, Zapier errors, Hosting provider failures).

**2.2. Liability Cap.**
In no event shall OnePoint CTO's total liability for all claims related to the Services exceed the **total amount of fees paid by the Client to OnePoint CTO during the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**.

**2.3. Automation & AI Disclaimer.**
Client acknowledges that the Services may involve the implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automated workflows. Provider makes no guarantees regarding the accuracy, reliability, or continuous availability of output generated by AI models (e.g., "hallucinations" or incorrect responses). Client accepts responsibility for reviewing and verifying critical outputs from these systems.

## 3. Third-Party Services & Authorization

**3.1. Agent Authorization.**
Client hereby authorizes OnePoint CTO to act as its agent for the limited purpose of:
*   Creating accounts on Client's behalf with third-party service providers (e.g., OpenAI, Twilio, SendGrid, Wave).
*   Accepting Terms of Service and Privacy Policies of such third-party providers on Client's behalf.
*   Managing billing and subscription settings using Client-provided payment methods.

**3.2. Independent Relationships.**
Client acknowledges that OnePoint CTO does not control these third-party providers. Any fees charged by these third parties are the sole responsibility of the Client and are separate from OnePoint CTO's service fees.
